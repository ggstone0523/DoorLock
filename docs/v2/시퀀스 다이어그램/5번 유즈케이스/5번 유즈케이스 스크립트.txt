title 6번 유즈케이스

actor "User" as user
participant "*도어락 외부 작동 잠금 버튼_INTERRUPT" as lockbtn
participant "lockdoor" as lockdoor
participant "motor" as motor
participant "sound" as sound
participant "numberkey" as numberkey

user->lockbtn:도어락 외부 작동 잠금 버튼을 누른다
lockbtn->lockdoor:TaskModeEventGroup[Event](TASKMODE_LOCKDOOR)
lockdoor->motor:MotorEventGroup[Event](MOTOR_STATUS_GET_FOR_LOOKDOOR)
motor->lockdoor:MotorStatusMessageQueue[MQ](uint8)
alt 시나리오(기본)-1
lockdoor->numberkey:LockEventGroup[Event](LOCK_UP)
lockdoor->sound:SoundEventGroup[Event](SOUND_SUCCESS)
else 시나리오(예외상황)-1
lockdoor->sound:SoundEventGroup[Event](SOUND_FAIL)
end
